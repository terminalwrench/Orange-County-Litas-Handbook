# Annual Goals

Use this document to track the chapter's annual leadership priorities. Goals should help leaders focus attention without turning every idea into a commitment.

## Annual Planning Areas

Consider goals for:

- Member welcome and retention
- Beginner rider support
- Event consistency
- Ride lead development
- Venue relationships
- Partnerships
- Social and media workflow
- Documentation and archive quality
- Leadership continuity

## Goal Format

For each goal, track:

- Goal
- Why it matters
- Owner
- Supporting roles
- Related module
- Target quarter
- Progress notes
- Completion signal

## Review Rhythm

Review annual goals during quarterly planning in [Quarterly-Planning.md](Quarterly-Planning.md). Adjust goals when chapter capacity, member needs, or safety considerations change.
