# Succession Planning

Succession planning helps the chapter continue smoothly when leadership roles change.

## Continuity Areas

Track:

- Role responsibilities
- Current owner
- Backup or support person
- Key contacts
- Recurring tasks
- Active projects
- Access or tool dependencies
- Documentation that needs updating

## Transition Checklist

- [ ] Confirm outgoing responsibilities.
- [ ] Confirm incoming owner.
- [ ] Transfer active action items.
- [ ] Review event assignments.
- [ ] Share relevant partner or venue context.
- [ ] Update role records.
- [ ] Update handbook references if needed.
- [ ] Schedule follow-up check-in.

## Privacy

Do not store passwords, private account access details, or sensitive personal context in this file.
