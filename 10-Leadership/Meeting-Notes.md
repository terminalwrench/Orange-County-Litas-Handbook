# Meeting Notes

Leadership meeting notes should capture decisions, ownership, and next steps. They do not need to transcribe every conversation.

Use [Templates/Leadership-Meeting.md](Templates/Leadership-Meeting.md) for each meeting.

## Standard Meeting Record

Capture:

- Date
- Attendees
- Purpose
- Topics discussed
- Decisions made
- Action items
- Event updates
- Member feedback themes
- Parking lot ideas
- Follow-up date

## After The Meeting

Within a reasonable follow-up window:

- Add decisions to [Decision-Log.md](Decision-Log.md).
- Add open tasks to [Action-Items.md](Action-Items.md).
- Update [Event-Assignments.md](Event-Assignments.md) when event ownership changes.
- Move future ideas to [Ideas-Parking-Lot.md](Ideas-Parking-Lot.md).
- Update production handbook modules only when guidance is final and reusable.

## Privacy

Summarize sensitive topics as operational themes. Do not store private conflict details, personal disclosures, or incident specifics in general meeting notes.
