# Decision Log

Use this log for internal leadership decisions that affect chapter operations, events, partnerships, communications, or handbook guidance.

Use [Templates/Decision-Record.md](Templates/Decision-Record.md) for detailed decisions.

## Decision Entry Format

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD - Decision Title

- Status:
- Owner:
- Context:
- Decision:
- Rationale:
- Affected modules:
- Follow-up:
```

## Status Values

- Proposed
- Approved
- Rejected
- Superseded
- Needs review

## When To Log A Decision

Log decisions when they affect:

- Event standards
- Ride planning expectations
- Leadership roles
- Partner or venue approach
- Social posting workflow
- Member-facing communication
- Archive or documentation practices

Small day-of choices do not need formal records unless they create a reusable lesson.
