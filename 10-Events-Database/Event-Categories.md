# Event Categories

Categories keep the Events Database searchable and consistent. Use the most specific category that describes the event's primary purpose.

## Meet & Greet

A social meetup intended for member connection, new rider welcome, chapter visibility, or recurring community rhythm. Meet & Greets may include food or casual hangs, but the main purpose is gathering rather than riding.

## Ride

A chapter ride where the route, destination, or riding experience is the central activity. Use this when the ride is not specifically marked beginner or intermediate.

## Beginner Ride

A ride planned for newer riders or explicitly described as beginner-friendly. These records should cross-reference beginner ride standards and route planning notes when available.

## Intermediate Ride

A ride requiring more rider confidence because of distance, highway use, splitting, technical roads, pace, or complexity.

## Photo Event

An event centered on photography, content gathering, art, shows, or visual documentation.

## Charity

A fundraiser, donation drive, benefit ride, or service-oriented event.

## Collaboration

An event planned with another Litas chapter, partner, sponsor, venue, creator, or community organization.

## Travel

An overnight, out-of-area, campout, destination trip, or long-distance event.

## Community

A broader motorcycle or local community event where OC Litas participation is documented but the event is not primarily a chapter ride or meetup.

## Training

A skills, safety, education, preparedness, or rider development event.

## Special Event

A seasonal, anniversary, holiday, themed, or one-off chapter event that does not fit the other categories cleanly.
