# Chapter Requirements

This page keeps local leaders oriented around chapter alignment. It is not a substitute for current requirements from The Litas.

## Local Operating Checklist

OC Litas should maintain:

- Clear local leadership ownership
- Safe and welcoming event practices
- Respectful use of global and chapter identity
- Accurate public event communication
- Responsible ride planning
- Consistent archive and record keeping
- Partner and sponsor communication that reflects well on the chapter

## Membership And Ride Expectations

When membership, eligibility, waivers, or ride participation requirements are involved, verify current official guidance. Local documents may explain how OC Litas organizes events, but global requirements should be checked at the source.

## Escalation

Escalate questions when they involve:

- Legal or waiver language
- Official brand usage
- Starting, changing, or representing chapter status
- Paid merch using official marks
- Sponsorships that imply global partnership
- Public conflict or safety incidents
