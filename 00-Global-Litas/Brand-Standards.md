# Brand Standards

This page explains how OC Litas handles global brand standards operationally. It does not recreate or replace official The Litas brand guidance.

## Principle

Global brand standards belong to The Litas. OC Litas should use official guidance and approved assets when representing the organization publicly.

## Local Responsibilities

Chapter leaders and media contributors should:

- Use official assets when required.
- Avoid altering official marks.
- Keep chapter-created materials clearly connected to approved chapter identity.
- Confirm partner-facing materials before they are shared publicly.
- Store reusable approved assets in [../03-Media/Assets](../03-Media/Assets/).

## When To Verify

Verify current official guidance before:

- Printing merch using Litas marks
- Sharing partner or sponsor materials
- Creating a new logo variation
- Using official marks on paid products
- Publishing materials that imply global endorsement

If the answer is not clear locally, escalate through chapter leadership before publishing.
