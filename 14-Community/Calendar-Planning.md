# Calendar Planning

Community events can create conflicts even when OC Litas is not hosting them. Use this guide during quarterly and monthly planning.

## Conflict Review

Before confirming an OC event, check:

- Other Litas chapter events.
- Major industry weekends.
- Charity rides and registration-based events.
- Holiday rides and seasonal gatherings.
- Training events.
- Travel-heavy weekends.
- Events likely to affect traffic, lodging, parking, or rider availability.

## Planning Questions

- Would many OC members likely attend the community event instead?
- Is the community event close enough that OC could ride to it?
- Would scheduling an OC event the same day split the chapter?
- Does the event create a better collaboration opportunity?
- Does the event require early lodging, registration, donation, or ticket decisions?
- Should OC keep that weekend open?

## Calendar Tags

Use consistent planning language:

- Awareness only.
- Informal attendance.
- Possible ride to event.
- Confirmed ride to event.
- Collaboration.
- Co-hosted.
