# Industry Events

Industry events can be useful for community connection, content, vendors, travel, and motorcycle culture. They can also consume rider availability and leadership capacity.

Use this guide during calendar planning and cross-reference [../12-Events-Database](../12-Events-Database/) when OC plans an official ride or attendance moment.

## Use

Leadership should decide whether each industry event is:

- A calendar note only.
- A social media awareness item.
- An informal member attendance opportunity.
- An official OC ride or meetup.
- A collaboration or partner opportunity.

If OC publishes a ride, meetup, or attendance plan, create a record in the Events Database rather than only updating this module.
