# Community Partners

Community partners are organizations, riding communities, venues, creators, and event hosts that appear in OC calendar history or documented event participation.

This file is a relationship map, not a sponsorship list. Formal sponsors and venue contacts should continue to live in [../06-Partnerships](../06-Partnerships/) and [../13-Venues](../13-Venues/).

## Relationship Notes

Before treating a community organization as a partner, confirm:

- Whether OC has an existing relationship or only a calendar-history reference.
- Who owns communication.
- Whether the event is public, invite-only, ticketed, donation-based, or registration-based.
- Whether OC is expected to promote, ride, volunteer, donate, or simply attend.
- Whether there are brand, safety, alcohol, or route concerns.

Document relationship changes in the relevant community event file and, if appropriate, in [../06-Partnerships](../06-Partnerships/).
