# Annual Events

Annual community events can shape the chapter calendar even when OC Litas is not the host. Leadership should review these events during quarterly planning so chapter rides, meetups, and collaborations do not compete with major community weekends.

## Planning Rhythm

- January: Review winter industry events, Paradise Road Show, Parts & Labor, and early-year Litas chapter anniversaries.
- March to April: Watch for Babes in Borrego and spring training or desert events.
- May: Review Distinguished Gentleman's Ride and International Women's Ride Day timing.
- June: Plan around Born Free and summer motorcycle events.
- August: Review beach days and multi-chapter summer gatherings.
- December: Watch for holiday rides, charity drives, and other chapter holiday events.

## Planning Notes

Major annual events can affect:

- Rider availability.
- Social media timing.
- Hotel and travel costs.
- Road traffic.
- Ride leader capacity.
- Partner availability.
- Chapter appetite for another event the same weekend.

If OC plans an official ride or meetup connected to an annual event, create or update the event record in [../12-Events-Database](../12-Events-Database/) and link it from the relevant community event record.
