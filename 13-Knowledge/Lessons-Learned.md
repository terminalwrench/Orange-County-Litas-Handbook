## Patterns to Watch

When the same lesson repeats, update the relevant operating document rather than only adding another note here.

Watch for:

- Repeated venue parking problems.
- Route difficulty being misunderstood.
- Public event copy missing key details.
- Volunteer roles being unclear.
- Weather decisions happening too late.
- First-time attendees not knowing who to look for.
- Photo, recap, or archive follow-up slipping after events.

## Maintenance

Each lesson should include:

- What happened.
- Why it mattered.
- What the chapter changed or should change.
- Where the operational standard now lives.
- Related event, route, venue, or community record.
