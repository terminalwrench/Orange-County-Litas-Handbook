# Financial Tracking

This file provides a basic recordkeeping framework for chapter money movement. It is not accounting, tax, legal, or financial advice.

Use this document only to keep practical records clear enough that leadership can understand what money came in, what money went out, who handled it, and what it was for.

## What to Track

Track money related to:

- Event costs.
- Donations.
- Raffles.
- Merchandise.
- Venue deposits.
- Reimbursements.
- Sponsor or partner contributions.
- Supplies.

## Record Fields

For each transaction, record:

- Date.
- Amount.
- Paid by or received from.
- Purpose.
- Related event or project.
- Payment method.
- Receipt or proof location.
- Reimbursement status.
- Notes.

## Basic Controls

- Keep receipts or screenshots.
- Avoid mixing unclear personal and chapter costs.
- Confirm reimbursement expectations before money is spent.
- Document cash handling carefully.
- Limit access to sensitive financial details.
- Review open reimbursements monthly.

## Related Records

- Link event-specific costs to [../10-Events-Database](../10-Events-Database/README.md) records when appropriate.
- Track sponsor or partner commitments in [../11-Venues](../11-Venues/README.md).
- Store private financial detail only in the appropriate restricted leadership location.

## Review

During monthly operations review:

- Confirm open reimbursements.
- Confirm event-related costs are documented.
- Confirm donation or raffle totals if applicable.
- Confirm receipts are stored.
- Identify unresolved questions.
