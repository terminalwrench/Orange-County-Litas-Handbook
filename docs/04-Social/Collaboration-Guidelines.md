# Collaboration Guidelines

Collaborations should be clear before content is posted. This includes partner venues, sponsors, photographers, other chapters, creators, charities, and motorcycle community groups.

## Confirm Before Posting

Confirm:

- Who owns the post
- Whether collaborator posting is expected
- Required tags
- Required logos
- Approved event details
- Caption approval needs
- Asset approval needs
- Posting date
- Cancellation authority

## Collaborator Posts

Use collaborator posts when both accounts should share the post publicly. Confirm the collaborator account, send the invite at publish time, and check that it is accepted.

## Credits

Credit photographers, venues, sponsors, and partners according to the agreement for the event. Do not imply sponsorship or endorsement where none exists.
