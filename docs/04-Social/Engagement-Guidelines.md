# Engagement Guidelines

Engagement should make people feel welcomed, informed, and respected.

## Responding

Respond when comments or messages ask about:

- Event details
- Ride difficulty
- Beginner friendliness
- Location
- Parking
- RSVP or cost
- Weather changes
- How to attend for the first time

If a question needs a leadership decision, acknowledge it and confirm privately before answering publicly.

## Sharing

When resharing attendee content:

- Credit the creator when possible.
- Avoid sharing unsafe riding behavior.
- Avoid posting private or sensitive moments.
- Respect requests not to share a photo or clip.

## Tone

Keep responses clear, warm, and practical. Do not argue publicly. Move sensitive issues to direct communication with leadership.
