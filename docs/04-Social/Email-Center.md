## When To Use Email

Use email when information needs to be easy to reference later, longer than an Instagram caption, or important enough that members may need the details outside social media.

Good uses include:

- New rider welcome messages.
- Ride announcements and route requirements.
- Meet and greet announcements.
- Event reminders.
- Monthly recaps.
- Sponsor or partner thank-you notes.
- General chapter communications.

## Send Standard

Before sending, verify:

- Date, time, and location.
- Venue name and address.
- Rider requirements.
- RSVP or registration instructions.
- Partner, sponsor, and photographer credits.
- Related event record in the [Events Database](../10-Events-Database/README.md).
- Final proofread.

## Keep It Free

This center is intentionally service-neutral. It can be used with a personal email account, shared chapter account, Instagram DM follow-up, or any future free tool leadership chooses.
