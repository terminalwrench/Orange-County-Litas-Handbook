# Community Management

Community management protects the chapter's public space and keeps communication useful.

## DMs

Direct messages should be checked regularly around event announcements.

Track:

- First-time attendee questions
- Ride difficulty questions
- Partner inquiries
- Sponsor inquiries
- Safety concerns
- Photo or repost requests

Do not store private personal details in public handbook files.

## Comments

Comments should be monitored after event announcements, cancellations, and recaps. Correct inaccurate information quickly and avoid letting conflicting answers spread.

## Moderation

Escalate comments or messages involving:

- Harassment
- Unsafe riding behavior
- Conflict between members
- Legal, waiver, or insurance questions
- Partner or sponsor complaints
- Incident details

Moderation decisions should protect member trust and chapter safety.
