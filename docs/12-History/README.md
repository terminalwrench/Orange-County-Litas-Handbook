This module preserves the durable history of Orange County Litas: how the chapter began, who helped lead it, which events shaped it, what traditions emerged, and what future leaders should understand about the community they are inheriting.

History is institutional knowledge. It helps the chapter honor the people who built it, remember meaningful events, understand leadership transitions, and carry traditions forward without depending on memory alone.

Use this module with the canonical [Events Database](../10-Events-Database/README.md), [Chapter History](../01-Chapter/Chapter-History.md), [Leadership](../01-Chapter/Leadership.md), [Venues](../11-Venues/README.md), and [Operations](../14-Operations/README.md).

## Historical Standard

Do not invent chapter history. Record confirmed facts, link to source records when available, and leave placeholders where future detail belongs.

Confirmed origin and leadership facts currently include:

- September 2023: Future founders met at Babe Ride Out Central Coast.
- October 2023: Orange County chapter founded by Jessica and J.
- Yvonne joined early leadership.
- Yvonne later relocated and stepped away.
- Rachel and Diana joined leadership.
- January 2026 leadership transition.
- Aly joined leadership.

Update history after major events, leadership transitions, anniversaries, milestone rides, collaborations, charity participation, campouts, and traditions that become part of chapter culture. Keep the visible record concise; use the [Events Database](../10-Events-Database/README.md) for detailed event-by-event history.
