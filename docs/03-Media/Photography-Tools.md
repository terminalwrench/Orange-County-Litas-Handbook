# Photography Tools

Use this page during event coverage and photo handoff. It is an operational checklist, not a creative standard.

## Event Shot List

- Venue or destination context.
- Bikes arriving.
- Group parking.
- First-timer welcome moments.
- Ride lead or host.
- Candid group interaction.
- Group photo.
- Partner or sponsor details.
- Safe bike details.
- Recap-ready closing images.

## Photo Shoot Reminders

- Confirm check-in flow.
- Confirm staging location.
- Capture individual rider portraits when planned.
- Capture bike portraits when planned.
- Capture detail shots.
- Note any delivery or credit requirements leadership should know.

## Delivery Checklist

- [ ] Final selects are saved.
- [ ] Photographer credit is confirmed.
- [ ] Social-ready images are shared with the publishing owner.
- [ ] Gallery or asset link is saved with the related event record when useful.
- [ ] Private payment or contact details are kept out of public portal files.
