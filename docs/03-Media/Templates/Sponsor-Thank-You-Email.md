# Sponsor Thank You Email

Use this when thanking a sponsor, donor, venue, or community partner.

## Subject

```text
Thank you from Orange County Litas
```

## Email Draft

```text
Hi [name],

Thank you for supporting Orange County Litas and [event or activity].

Your support helped us [brief outcome or benefit].

We appreciate your time, generosity, and connection with the riding community.

Thank you again,
Orange County Litas
```

## Send Checks

- [ ] Sponsor or partner name is correct.
- [ ] Event name is correct.
- [ ] Any promised deliverables or tags are confirmed.
- [ ] Related event record is linked when useful.
- [ ] Final proofread completed.
