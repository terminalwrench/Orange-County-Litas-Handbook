# Meet and Greet Email Template

Use this when announcing or reminding members about a meet and greet.

## Subject

```text
[OC Litas] Meet & Greet - [Date]
```

## Email Draft

```text
Hi everyone,

Our next Orange County Litas meet and greet is coming up.

Date:
Time:
Venue:
Address:
Parking notes:

This is a casual meetup. New riders are welcome, and bikes are not required unless the event details say otherwise.

Please check the event post for the latest details before heading out.

Orange County Litas
```

## Send Checks

- [ ] Venue and address verified.
- [ ] Parking notes included.
- [ ] Reservation or capacity notes included when needed.
- [ ] Related venue record checked.
- [ ] Final proofread completed.
