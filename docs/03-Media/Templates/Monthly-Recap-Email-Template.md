# Monthly Recap Email Template

Use this to summarize chapter activity at the end of a month.

## Subject

```text
[OC Litas] [Month] Recap
```

## Email Draft

```text
Hi everyone,

Here is a quick look back at [month].

Highlights:
- [Highlight]
- [Highlight]
- [Highlight]

Thank you to:
- [Partner, venue, volunteer, photographer, or ride lead]

Coming up:
- [Upcoming event]
- [Upcoming event]

Orange County Litas
```

## Recap Checks

- [ ] Event names match the Events Database.
- [ ] Photographer and partner credits are included.
- [ ] Upcoming dates are verified.
- [ ] Links are current.
- [ ] Final proofread completed.
