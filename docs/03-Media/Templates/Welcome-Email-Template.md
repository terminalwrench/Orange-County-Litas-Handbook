# Welcome Email Template

Use this when welcoming a new rider or prospective member into the chapter.

## Subject

```text
Welcome to Orange County Litas
```

## Email Draft

```text
Hi [name],

Welcome to Orange County Litas. We are glad you found us.

A good first step is to review the New Rider Guide:
[link]

Upcoming events are shared through our normal chapter channels. If you are joining a ride, please read the ride details carefully and message leadership if you are unsure whether the route is a fit for your current comfort level.

We are happy to have you here.

Orange County Litas
```
