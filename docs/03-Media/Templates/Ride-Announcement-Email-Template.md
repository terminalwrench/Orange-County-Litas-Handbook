# Ride Announcement Email Template

Use this when sharing ride details with members.

## Subject

```text
[OC Litas] Ride Details - [Ride Name] - [Date]
```

## Email Draft

```text
Hi everyone,

Here are the details for our upcoming ride.

Ride:
Date:
Meet time:
Kickstands up:
Meet location:
Approximate distance:
Estimated ride time:
Difficulty:
Fuel stop:
Restroom stop:
Food stop:

Rider requirements:
- [Requirement]
- [Requirement]

Please arrive fueled up and ready for the pre-ride briefing.

Orange County Litas
```

## Send Checks

- [ ] Route details match the approved route plan.
- [ ] Difficulty is clear.
- [ ] Fuel, restroom, and regroup details are included.
- [ ] Rider requirements are clear.
- [ ] Weather and contingency notes are included when needed.
- [ ] Final proofread completed.
