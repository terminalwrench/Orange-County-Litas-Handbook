## After The Meeting

Within a reasonable follow-up window:

- Add open tasks to [Action-Items.md](Action-Items.md).
- Move future ideas to [Ideas-Parking-Lot.md](Ideas-Parking-Lot.md).
- Update [Quarterly-Planning.md](Quarterly-Planning.md) if the calendar or priorities changed.
- Update production handbook modules only when guidance is final and reusable.

## Privacy

Summarize sensitive topics as operational themes. Do not store private conflict details, personal disclosures, or incident specifics in general meeting notes.
