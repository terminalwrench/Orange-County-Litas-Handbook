# Leadership Roles

This document defines how OC Litas leadership roles are documented internally. It does not replace the public chapter guidance in [../01-Chapter/Leadership.md](../01-Chapter/Leadership.md).

## Role Record

For each role, track:

- Role name
- Current owner
- Backup or support person
- Core responsibilities
- Recurring tasks
- Decision authority
- Key documents or systems used
- Transition notes

## Common Responsibility Areas

Leadership responsibilities may include:

- Chapter coordination
- Event planning
- Ride lead coordination
- Social and communications
- Media and asset coordination
- Partnerships and venues
- Member welcome and feedback
- Archive and documentation maintenance

One person may hold multiple responsibilities, but each responsibility should have clear ownership.

## Role Changes

When responsibilities change:

- Update the role record.
- Transfer current action items.
- Share relevant contacts and notes.
- Update event assignments where needed.
- Capture transition details in [../14-Operations/Leadership-Handoff.md](../14-Operations/Leadership-Handoff.md).

## Privacy

Keep role documentation practical. Avoid storing private member issues or sensitive personal context in role descriptions.
