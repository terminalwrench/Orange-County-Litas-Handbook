# Quarterly Planning

Quarterly Planning is the leadership hub for turning the chapter calendar into realistic work. Open this page when planning a quarter, checking upcoming commitments, or deciding whether the team has capacity for another event.

Use [Templates/Quarterly-Review.md](Templates/Quarterly-Review.md) at the end of each quarter.

Before adding new work, review the current calendar and confirm:

- Existing meet and greets.
- Planned rides.
- Community events or collaborations.
- Leadership availability.
- Venue or route dependencies.
- Media and social deadlines.

## Quarterly Inputs

Review:

- Annual goals
- Upcoming events
- Event recaps and lessons learned
- Open action items
- Partnership opportunities
- Route and ride lead needs
- Documentation updates

## Quarterly Plan

For each quarter, define:

- Priority focus
- Key events
- Leadership assignments
- Risks or capacity constraints
- Communication needs
- Handbook updates needed
- Review date

## Capacity Check

Before approving the quarter, confirm:

- Each event has an owner.
- Each ride has a likely lead and sweep.
- Media and social work has enough lead time.
- Venue and route uncertainty is documented.
- No major community event conflicts are obvious.
- Follow-up work is captured in [Action-Items.md](Action-Items.md).
