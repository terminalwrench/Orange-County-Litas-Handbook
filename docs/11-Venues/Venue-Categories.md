# Venue Categories

Venue categories keep the database searchable and help leadership choose locations that match the event format.

## Breweries

Use for breweries, taprooms, and beer-focused venues. Review parking, seating, non-alcoholic options, food availability, and whether the venue works for riders who are not drinking.

## Coffee

Use for coffee shops, cafes, and morning meetup stops. Review morning hours, parking turnover, restroom access, seating, and whether the location can handle first-time attendees arriving separately.

## Restaurants

Use for food-first venues, lunch stops, dinner stops, and ride destinations centered on a meal. Review seating, wait times, reservations, parking, payment flow, and group arrival timing.

## Bars

Use for saloons, bars, and nightlife-oriented venues. Review age restrictions, parking, food availability, noise, rider safety, and whether the event should be social-only or include a ride.

## Beaches

Use for beaches, coastal access points, and beach-day locations. Review parking cost, seasonal crowding, sand, pedestrian activity, gear storage, restrooms, shade, and holiday traffic.

## Wineries

Use for wineries and vineyard destinations. Review distance, reservations, parking surface, food availability, rider expectations, non-alcoholic options, and whether the ride should be destination-only for some attendees.

## Misc

Use when a documented location does not fit another category or when the calendar source does not provide enough detail to classify the venue confidently. Update the category after leadership confirms more information.
