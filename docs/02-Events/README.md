Events is the operational home for both upcoming work and past records. Planning tools live here, and completed events are preserved in the [Events Index](../10-Events-Database/Events-Index.md).

Use the site search when looking for a specific venue, ride name, collaboration, month, or event title.

## Event Workflow

Every event should move through the same basic lifecycle:

1. Idea
2. Planning
3. Venue confirmation
4. Public details
5. Ride planning, if applicable
6. Event execution
7. Recap and record update

The [Event Lifecycle](Event-Lifecycle.md) keeps planning clear without making every event feel formal. A casual meet and greet may move through the process quickly. A destination ride, collaboration, beach day, charity event, or larger gathering needs more owners and more confirmation.

## Operating Standard

Every OC Litas event should answer four questions before it is announced:

- What is happening?
- Who is it for?
- What should attendees expect?
- Who is responsible for day-of decisions?

For ride events, leaders must also answer:

- Is this beginner friendly?
- What is the route style and pace?
- Where are gas, restroom, and regroup points?
- Who is leading and who is sweeping?
- What is the weather and cancellation plan?

Good event operations should feel calm from the outside. Members should see clear details, warm communication, and a well-hosted event. Behind the scenes, leaders should have enough structure to make confident decisions when timing, weather, venue traffic, or group size changes.
