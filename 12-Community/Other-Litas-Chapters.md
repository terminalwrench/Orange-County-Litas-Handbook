## Collaboration Standard

When participating in another chapter's event:

- Confirm whether OC is attending informally, planning a ride to the event, or co-hosting.
- Respect the host chapter's timing, rules, route, and public communication.
- Avoid duplicating their announcement without permission.
- Link official OC participation back to the Events Database.
- Capture future notes after the event so the relationship history is not lost.
