# Delivery Workflow

Delivery workflow depends on the event type.

## Event Photos

For standard events:

- Save final selects.
- Confirm photographer credit.
- Share social-ready images.
- Archive gallery or asset link.

## Paid Or Scheduled Shoots

For scheduled sessions:

- Track delivery contact outside public files.
- Confirm payment status outside public files.
- Send final gallery.
- Record delivery date.
- Archive non-private delivery notes.
