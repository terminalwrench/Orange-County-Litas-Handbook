# Photography Archive Workflow

Photography archives should preserve final usable work without burying future leaders in unreviewed files.

## Archive

Keep:

- Final selects
- Gallery link
- Photographer credit
- Usage notes
- Related event recap
- Delivery status when relevant

Avoid storing:

- Private delivery contact details
- Payment records
- Sensitive incident images
- Unreviewed duplicates with no future use

Use [../09-Archive/README.md](../09-Archive/README.md) for long-term archive standards.
