## Naming

- Use clear, descriptive filenames.
- Prefer dates for event-specific archive folders.
- Keep templates generic.
- Avoid saving working drafts as permanent records unless they explain a major decision.

## Updating Records

When updating a record:

- Add the new information where it belongs.
- Link related files.
- Avoid duplicating the same full content across modules.
- Leave unknown details blank rather than guessing.
- Remove stale placeholders only when replaced by real information.

## Review Cycle

At least quarterly, review:

- Broken links.
- Empty placeholders that need attention.
- Duplicate or outdated guidance.
- Event records missing recaps.
- Venue, route, or partner records that need updates.
