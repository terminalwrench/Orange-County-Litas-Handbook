# Sponsors

Sponsors provide money, goods, services, prizes, venue support, or promotional support for a chapter event or initiative.

## Sponsor Record

Record:

- Sponsor name
- Contact person
- Contribution
- Event or campaign
- Required logo or tag
- Public thank-you requirement
- Deliverables owed
- Follow-up date

Keep private contact details in an appropriate internal location, not in public-facing files.

## Sponsor Handling

Before publishing sponsor materials, confirm asset approval, required language, and whether the sponsor relationship is event-specific or ongoing.
