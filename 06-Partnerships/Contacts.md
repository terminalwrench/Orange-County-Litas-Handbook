# Partnership Contacts

Use this document to define how partnership contacts should be maintained. Store sensitive contact details only in an appropriate internal location.

## Contact Categories

- Venues
- Sponsors
- Photographers
- Vendors
- Charities
- Collaborating chapters
- Community partners

## Contact Notes

For each contact, track:

- Name or organization
- Role
- Best use
- Communication preference
- Last interaction
- Follow-up needed

Do not publish personal phone numbers, private emails, or payment details in broad handbook files.
