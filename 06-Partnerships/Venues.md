# Venues

Venues support meet and greets, ride destinations, photo events, fundraisers, and special events.

## Venue Record

Track:

- Venue name
- Address
- Contact
- Best event type
- Parking notes
- Reservation notes
- Food or drink notes
- Photography notes
- Group size fit
- Follow-up history

Use [../02-Events/Venue-Selection.md](../02-Events/Venue-Selection.md) before adding a venue to a major event.
