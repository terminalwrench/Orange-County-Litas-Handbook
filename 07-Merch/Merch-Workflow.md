# Merch Workflow

Use this workflow for patches, stickers, apparel, and other OC Litas merchandise.

## Workflow

1. Define purpose and audience.
2. Confirm whether official or chapter marks are used.
3. Verify approval needs.
4. Prepare artwork and production specs.
5. Get vendor quote.
6. Confirm cost, quantity, and payment process.
7. Approve proof.
8. Receive order.
9. Track distribution.
10. Archive final art, vendor notes, and order details.

Use [../03-Media/Naming-Conventions.md](../03-Media/Naming-Conventions.md) for files and [../09-Archive/README.md](../09-Archive/README.md) for preservation.
