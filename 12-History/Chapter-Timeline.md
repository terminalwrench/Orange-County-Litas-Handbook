# Chapter Timeline

This timeline records confirmed Orange County Litas history and links to event records where available.

## 2023

- September 2023: Future founders met at Babe Ride Out Central Coast.
- October 2023: Orange County chapter founded by Jessica and J.
- October 19, 2023: First documented OC Litas meet and greet in the Events Database: [OC Litas Meet & Greet 7pm](../10-Events-Database/2023/2023-10-19-oc-litas-meet-and-greet-7pm.md).
- October 21, 2023: First documented OC Litas ride in the Events Database: [Oak Glen Ride](../10-Events-Database/2023/2023-10-21-oak-glen-ride.md).
- October 28, 2023: First documented beginner ride in the Events Database: [Babes and brew beginners ride](../10-Events-Database/2023/2023-10-28-babes-and-brew-beginners-ride.md).
- November to December 2023: Early community meetups and rides continued, including South Moto OC events and the holiday meet and greet.
