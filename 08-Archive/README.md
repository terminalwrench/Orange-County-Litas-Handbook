# Archive

The archive preserves completed OC Litas work so future leaders can understand what happened, reuse what is useful, and avoid repeating preventable problems.

## What Belongs In The Archive

Archive:

- Final event briefs
- Final flyers and captions
- Recaps
- Route notes
- Venue notes
- Photo and video links
- Partner deliverables
- Merch production records
- Lessons learned

Do not archive broad drafts, private contact details, payment records, or sensitive incident information in general handbook folders.

## Archive Standard

Use date-based folders:

```text
yyyy-mm-dd-event-name
```

Each archive record should make clear:

- What happened
- Who owned it
- What was published or delivered
- What assets exist
- What should change next time

## Maintenance

Review archives periodically for broken links, unclear filenames, duplicate files, and reusable assets that should move into [../03-Media/Assets](../03-Media/Assets/).
