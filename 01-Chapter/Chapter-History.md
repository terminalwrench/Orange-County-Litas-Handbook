# Chapter History

OC Litas history is worth preserving because the chapter is built from people, rides, places, creative work, leadership effort, and moments that are easy to lose if no one writes them down.

This document is the chapter's long-term memory. It should capture enough context that future leaders understand where traditions came from, which events shaped the community, and how the Orange County chapter grew over time.

## Why Preserve History

Preserving chapter history helps OC Litas:

- Honor the people who built and sustained the chapter.
- Remember important rides, events, venues, and collaborations.
- Understand why certain traditions exist.
- Avoid repeating past planning mistakes.
- Track leadership transitions with care.
- Give new members a sense of continuity.
- Support future anniversary posts, event recaps, and creative projects.

History should be factual, generous, and useful. It does not need to record every small detail, but it should capture the moments that shaped the chapter.
