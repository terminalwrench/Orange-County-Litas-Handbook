# Beach Routes

Beach routes need extra attention to traffic, parking, heat, wind, and gear storage.

## Planning Checks

Confirm:

- Motorcycle parking
- Car parking for non-riders
- Arrival time
- Beach access
- Restrooms
- Shade
- Water
- Wind conditions
- Gear and helmet plan

## Ride Fit

Beach routes can be relaxed destinations, but they may involve crowded streets and difficult parking. Review the route before labeling it beginner friendly.
