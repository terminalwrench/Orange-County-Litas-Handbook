# Coffee Routes

Coffee routes are short destination rides built around an accessible stop with parking, restrooms, and enough space for a casual group.

## Coffee Stop Criteria

Look for:

- Easy motorcycle parking
- Morning hours
- Restrooms
- Water access
- Seating or nearby gathering space
- Low-stress arrival and departure
- Friendly staff or group tolerance

## Planning

Coffee routes work well for beginner rides, casual weekend meetups, and first-time attendee events when route difficulty is kept honest.
