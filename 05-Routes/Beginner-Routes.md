# Beginner Routes

Beginner routes should reduce pressure and build confidence. Use this with [../02-Events/Beginner-Rides.md](../02-Events/Beginner-Rides.md).

## Route Criteria

Beginner routes should have:

- Predictable turns
- Manageable distance
- Clear regroup points
- Simple parking
- Gas nearby
- Low-pressure destination option
- Minimal freeway complexity

## Review Notes

Before using a beginner route, confirm current construction, traffic patterns, weather, and parking. A route that was beginner-friendly in one season may not be beginner-friendly during heavy traffic, heat, rain, or road work.
