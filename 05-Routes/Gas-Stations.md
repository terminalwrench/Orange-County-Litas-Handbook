# Gas Stations

Gas planning prevents ride stress and helps leaders keep timing realistic.

## Gas Stop Criteria

Good gas stops have:

- Enough pump access
- Safe entry and exit
- Room for bikes to gather without blocking traffic
- Restrooms when possible
- Nearby regroup space
- Clear route connection

## Ride Use

For longer rides, include planned gas stops in the ride plan. For local rides, public copy should usually remind riders to arrive fueled and ready.
