# Roadmap

This roadmap tracks handbook improvements that should happen as the chapter uses the system.

## Near Term

- Add real route records after each route is tested.
- Add venue notes after recurring event locations are reviewed.
- Add partner and sponsor records only after permission and contact expectations are clear.
- Keep media and social templates aligned with event workflow changes.

## Ongoing

- Review the handbook quarterly for stale links, outdated workflows, and missing lessons learned.
- Move repeated event learnings into permanent operating documents.
- Keep private contact information out of general handbook files.
- Preserve completed work in [08-Archive](08-Archive/) using the archive standards.
