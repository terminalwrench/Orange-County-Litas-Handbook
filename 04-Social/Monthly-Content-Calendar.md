# Monthly Content Calendar

The monthly calendar helps OC Litas balance event promotion, recaps, community posts, partner support, and reminders.

## Monthly Review

At the start of each month, review:

- Confirmed events
- Tentative events
- Recaps still owed
- Partner obligations
- Member-facing reminders
- Seasonal ride or weather considerations
- Media assets needed

## Calendar Fields

Track:

- Publish date
- Channel
- Post type
- Owner
- Related event
- Asset status
- Caption status
- Tags or collaborators
- Archive status

## Cadence

Use a light but reliable rhythm:

- Announce confirmed events early enough for planning.
- Share reminders close to the event.
- Post recaps while the event still feels current.
- Avoid stacking too many unrelated announcements on the same day.
