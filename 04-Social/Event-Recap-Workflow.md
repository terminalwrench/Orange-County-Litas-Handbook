# Event Recap Workflow

Recap posts help close the event loop and preserve chapter momentum.

## Gather

Collect:

- Final photos or video
- Attendance estimate
- Venue or partner credits
- Photographer credit
- Volunteer thanks
- Donation or prize results if applicable
- Link to the event recap

## Publish

The recap should thank attendees, acknowledge partners or venues when appropriate, and point people toward the next relevant chapter action.

## Archive

Save:

- Final recap caption
- Final media
- Posting date
- Tagged accounts
- Gallery or video link

Connect the social recap to [../02-Events/Event-Recap.md](../02-Events/Event-Recap.md).
